African Treasures

Site Planning Document

Site Name

Site Name: African Treasures

This name reflects a website dedicated to showcasing the rich cultural, natural, and historical treasures found throughout the African continent. It invites users to explore and appreciate Africa's beauty and diversity.

Optional domain availability: africantreasures.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the African Treasures website is to educate and inspire visitors by providing curated content about African landmarks, wildlife, traditions, and destinations. The site also promotes sustainable tourism and cultural appreciation.

Scenarios

  • What are some must-see heritage sites when visiting Africa?
  • How can I learn about different African cultures and their traditions?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #2F4F4F (Dark Slate Gray) - Used for headings and the footer background.
  • Accent Color: #DAA520 (Goldenrod) - Used for highlights, buttons, and borders.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Merriweather', serif - Used for main titles and section headings.
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Used for paragraph content and navigation links.
